Hello there, fellow traveller

Chekin is our attempt to create something magical – a way to experience the best parts of India through charming homestays, forest hideouts, and lovely villas by the beach.

We love that each of these places has its own blend of history, culture and a healthy dash of quirkiness; and we’d like to help you experience just that!
